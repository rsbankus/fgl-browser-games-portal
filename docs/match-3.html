Match-3 Browser Games

Match-3 games are puzzle experiences built around pattern recognition, planning, and satisfying chain reactions. They are popular with adult players looking for relaxed yet engaging gameplay.

Common Mechanics

  • Tile swapping and matching
  • Combo chains and cascades
  • Limited-move or timed levels
  • Seasonal or themed boards

A curated selection of match-3 titles can be found in the Match-3 category on FGL, featuring browser-based games playable on desktop and mobile.
