# Puzzle Games

Puzzle games emphasize logic, spatial reasoning, and problem-solving.
These games are well-suited for adult players who enjoy mental challenges
and short, focused play sessions.

This category includes number puzzles, hidden-object games, logic challenges,
and experimental mechanics.

The full collection is available in the
<a href="https://fgl.com/tags/puzzle/">Puzzle category on FGL</a>,
covering a wide range of difficulty levels and styles.
