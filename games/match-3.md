# Match-3 Games

Match-3 games focus on pattern recognition, planning, and satisfying
chain reactions. They are popular with adult players looking for
relaxing yet engaging gameplay.

Common mechanics include tile swapping, combo chains, limited-move
levels, and themed boards.

Players can browse and play these games in the
<a href="https://fgl.com/tags/match-3/">Match-3 category on FGL</a>,
which features a rotating selection of browser-based titles.
